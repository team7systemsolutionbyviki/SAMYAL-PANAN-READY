The Legacy of Samayal Panna Ready

We believe that great cooking starts with the highest quality ingredients. Founded in South India, our brand bridges the gap between busy modern lifestyles and traditional slow-cooked flavors. We hand-select premium poultry and grind our spices stone-cold to keep their essential oils intact.

2021

Started in Coimbatore

Launched with just 4 marinated chicken cuts, delivering locally to nearby communities with a hygiene first promise.

2022

In-House Spice Factory

Set up traditional slow stone-grinders to produce aromatic masala powders without adding artificial coloring or MSG.

2023

Cold Chain Expansion

Invested in refrigerated delivery bags and sub-zero storage systems ensuring absolute freshness of raw marinated meats.

2025

10,000+ Happy Families

Now the most preferred brand for ready-to-cook chicken and premium spices, loved by home cooks and foodies.
